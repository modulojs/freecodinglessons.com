Smoothie Recipe

Description

This recipe is great for a refreshing and filling on-the-go light meal.

Ingredients

Fruit

2 chopped bananas

1 cup blueberries

1 sliced apple

Liquid

1 cup soy milk

Instructions

Steps Step 1

Combine in blender.

Step 2

Blend for several minutes or until desired consistency.

Step 3

Enjoy!

Serving

Makes one serving, great as breakfast!
