竹間 淳 /// JUNE CHIKUMA /// ///

June Chikuma (竹間 淳, Chikuma Jun) has composed for TV, film, and video games over the past thirty plus years.

bomberman /// ボンバーマン

Her proto-techno and drum and bass soundtracks for Nintendo's Bomberman franchise in the 80s and 90s is an oeuvre unto itself.

the archives /// Les Archives

In more recent years her musical focus has turned toward classic Arabic and Egyptian music. Chikuma studies Arabic nay, playing and performing with Le Club Bachraf ensemble.

/// Le Club Bachraf ///

For Les Archives, Chikuma constructed a sonic stage to animate her imaginings as a one-person show.
