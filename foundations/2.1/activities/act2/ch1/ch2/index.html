Let's explore the biographies of a few brilliant mathematicians in computer history.

Charles Babbage and Ada Lovelace were close friends and co-created many early (theoretical) computer programs. Ada envisioned an idea of programming that pushed further, and theorized that computer programming could eventually automate factories, or play music. She was right. Alan Turing's brilliant mathematical of a computer would prove the full extent of this branch of mathematics possible, about 100 years later.
